What is Randoro?

Randoro is a modern Pomodoro timer that gamifies your focus sessions. You can choose classic mode for fixed durations, or random mode for unpredictable session lengths, making every focus session exciting.

Random Mode

Each Pomodoro duration is randomly generated between your chosen minimum and maximum. Breaks adjust based on the Pomodoro length. Avoid too small values like 0.01–0.02 minutes, which round to a few seconds.

Negative Timer / Stopwatch

Once a Pomodoro ends, you can continue counting down into negative time to stay in the zone without interruptions. Ideal for deep focus sessions.

Custom Sounds

You can upload your own alarm sound in the Notification Sound panel. This allows you to use any MP3 file – from motivational speeches to funny clips. Uploaded sounds are stored locally in your browser, so they’ll still work even after you close and reopen Randoro.
Note: Clearing browser cache/site data will remove custom sounds.

Tasks & New Game

You can add up to 3 focus(Poomdoro) tasks at a time. Once completed, mark them off and free up space for the next.
You can add unlimited amount of tasks in Day List, and move them to Pomodoro Tasks when there is space.

If you want to start fresh, use the New Game button. This clears your tasks and cycles, sessions, but keeps your sound settings.
