What is Randoro?

Randoro is a modern Pomodoro timer that gamifies your focus sessions. You can choose classic mode for fixed durations, or random mode for unpredictable session lengths, making every focus session exciting.

Random Mode

Each Pomodoro duration is randomly generated between your chosen minimum and maximum. Breaks adjust based on the Pomodoro length. Avoid too small values like 0.01â€“0.02 minutes, which round to a few seconds.

Negative Timer / Stopwatch

Once a Pomodoro ends, you can continue counting down into negative time to stay in the zone without interruptions. Ideal for deep focus sessions.
